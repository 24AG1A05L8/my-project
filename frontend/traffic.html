Traffic Rules in India

Helmet Rule

Wearing a helmet is mandatory for two-wheeler riders and pillion riders. Fine applies if violated.

Seat Belt Rule

Seat belts are compulsory for both driver and front-seat passengers.

No Drunk and Drive

Driving under the influence of alcohol is a punishable offense with heavy fines and imprisonment.

Speed Limits

Vehicles must follow speed limits specified for city roads and highways.
